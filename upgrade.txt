How to uprade from SmartMailLight
=================================

In order to upgrade the module, you will need to :

- Upload the complete /smartmaillight folder into the /modules directory of your website

- Then, log in to your site as administrator, go to System Admin > Modules, look for the SmartMailLight icon in the list of installed modules and click in the update icon

That's it !

Enjoy !
